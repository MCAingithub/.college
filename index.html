SSIT COLLEGE TUMKUR

SSIT - Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology is a premier institute established in 1979.
The institute is located in Tumkur, Karnataka and it offers Degree courses including UG and PG programs.
These programs are offered in Full-Time mode and are delivered by highly experienced faculty. Candidates can pursue education in multiple courses like MCA, B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech.
